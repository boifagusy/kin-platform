=== SkeletonLoader Usage Examples ===

Import:
import SkeletonLoader from "../components/ui/SkeletonLoader";

1. Contact List Loading:
<SkeletonLoader type="list" count={5} />

2. Dashboard Loading:
<SkeletonLoader type="dashboard" />

3. Card Loading:
<SkeletonLoader type="card" count={3} />

4. Alert Loading:
<SkeletonLoader type="alert" count={2} />

5. Text Loading:
<SkeletonLoader type="text" />

6. Circle Loading:
<SkeletonLoader type="circle" className="w-16 h-16" />

7. Rectangle Loading:
<SkeletonLoader type="rectangle" className="h-40" />

Custom className always works:
<SkeletonLoader type="card" className="my-2 mx-4" />
